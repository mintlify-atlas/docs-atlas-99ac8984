## About the project

Low-Poly Ocean is a scroll-driven interactive experience that takes users on a visual journey through the ocean. Each of the 10 endangered marine species found in Thai waters is rendered as a unique low-poly polygon artwork — built entirely from CSS `clip-path` polygon shards.

The project uses the fragmented, geometric nature of low-poly art to communicate a conservation message: just as removing polygon shards breaks the animal's image, losing a species breaks the entire ecosystem.
